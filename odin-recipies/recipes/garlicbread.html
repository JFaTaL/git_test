Garlic Bread Recipe

By James Julian, June 15th, 2024

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of French or Italian bread
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Slice the bread: Cut the loaf of bread in half lengthwise.
  3. Make the garlic butter: In a medium bowl, mix together the softened butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley, salt (if using), and black pepper until well combined.
  4. Spread the garlic butter: Evenly spread the garlic butter mixture over the cut sides of the bread.
  5. Add Parmesan cheese: If desired, sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese evenly over the buttered bread.
  6. Bake the bread: Place the bread halves on a baking sheet, cut side up, and bake in the preheated oven for about 10-15 minutes, or until the bread is golden and the butter is melted and bubbly.
  7. Optional broil: For extra crispy garlic bread, you can broil the bread for an additional 1-2 minutes, keeping a close eye to prevent burning.
  8. Serve: Remove the bread from the oven, let it cool slightly, then slice and serve warm.
