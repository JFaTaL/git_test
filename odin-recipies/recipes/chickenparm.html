Chicken Parmesan Recipe

By James Julian, June 15th, 2024

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 40 minutes

Total Time: 1 hour

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

Sauce

  • 3 Tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 small yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 6 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 (28-oz.) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 large sprigs basil
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Chicken

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs (Italian seasoned)
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Cooking Instructions:

Sauce

  1. Heat olive oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add onion and sauté until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add garlic and cook for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. If you like a slightly sweeter sauce, add the sugar.
  5. Bring the sauce to a simmer and let it cook for about 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. Remove from heat and set aside.

Chicken

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Set up a breading station with three shallow bowls: one with flour, one with beaten eggs, and one with a mixture of breadcrumbs and grated Parmesan cheese.
  3. Dredge each chicken breast in the flour, shaking off excess, then dip in the egg, and finally coat in the breadcrumb mixture.
  4. Heat olive oil in a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the breaded chicken breasts and cook until golden brown on both sides, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  5. Remove the skillet from heat and spoon about 2 tablespoons of marinara sauce over each chicken breast.
  6. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the chicken breasts.
  7. Transfer the skillet to the oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired and serve with additional marinara sauce on the side.
